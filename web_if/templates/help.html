GAME MODES

  • MAP - Guess the country from its map outline
  • FLAG - Guess the country from its national flag
  • CAP - Guess the capital city of a country

HOW TO PLAY

  1. Select a game mode (Map, Flag, or Capital)
  2. You have 30 seconds to answer each question
  3. Choose from multiple options to select your answer
  4. Your score increases with each correct answer

PLAYING AS GUEST

When playing as a guest:

  • You can play all game modes
  • Your scores will not be saved to the leaderboard
  • Your session ends when you close the browser
